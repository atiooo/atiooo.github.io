Hello there

I graduated from UC San Diego on 2018, where I am studying Design, Computer Science and Mathematics. I am continuing my journey in interaction design at CCA!

As a first-generation college student, my grandparents' lack of technological experience inspired me to explore various technology fields. Simple tasks my generation takes for granted seem like insurmountable challenges.

The mismatch that people with disabilities encounter is an epitome of the problems that everyone may face. By including elders, we can extend our design to reach more people who may encounter situational or temporary exclusion. I aim to build experiences that unify people and embrace individuality.
